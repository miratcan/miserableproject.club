Miserable Projects Directory is a space where solopreneurs and tiny teams (no more than 3 people) share their failed side projects. No investor decks, no polished success stories -- just real attempts, burned weekends, wasted domains, and a few hard-earned lessons.

We are building a clean, honest archive that anyone can read, but only logged-in users can contribute. You can post anonymously. No comments. No likes. Just people who have actually tried -- and failed -- showing up to share what they learned.

This site may look unfinished -- that is intentional. We want it to grow together with its community. If you would like to suggest features, report issues, or even build something new, visit our GitHub page and open an issue.

If you have crashed a few projects yourself, you are in the right place to cry together.
